Q: But if there are 597 prizes, how are there 950 laureates?

A: The statutes of the Nobel Foundation allow for the division of prizes:

A prize amount may be equally divided between two works, each of which is considered to merit a prize.
For collaborators on a work, the prize shall be awarded to them jointly.
In no case may a prize amount be divided between more than three persons.
